Mission

To promote Fair Trade and provide philanthropic solutions for communities in need on a small to medium scale. Targeting schools and shelters from varying local towns to ensure fair and impactful change!





"Some may call this junk, me I call them treasures."

Reduce Outsourcing

With WillGood, you barter clothing instead of spending money on expensive clothing brands who may use unethical business practices!

Provide Clothing to Those in Need

Any unbartered clothing gets donated to nearby homeless shelters and those in need! Or you can mark any clothing that you dont want bartered and will immediately be added to our donation bin!

Promote Apparel Reuse

Over 500 Million Kilograms of unwanted clothing are tossed into landfills! (WHAT A WASTE) By donating the clothing you dont want, who's to say nobody will want it at all? Lets reduce waste and provide for eachother!

How you can
Help

By participating in our charity drives, you are providing clothes to those who are less fortunate! By bartering with WillGood you offer an opportunity for people who need clothes but can't afford it while at the same time avoiding purchasing clothes from unethical fast fashion companies! If the clothing at WillGood don't suit your liking but you still want to support fair trade clothing, THERE ARE MORE OPTIONS!! Click "More Info" to be directed to a website that provides 35 different clothing brands that support fair trade clothing!
